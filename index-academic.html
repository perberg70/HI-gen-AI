Academic Edition

Working with AI in Academia?

Experience the synergy of human creativity and the power of AI. This course empowers academic staff to responsibly leverage Generative AI to enhance research, teaching, and administrative processes. You will learn how to tackle obstacles, manage risks associated with AI usage in academia, and use AI to your advantage while maintaining academic integrity.

Why This Course, Why Now?

  • The rapid evolution of AI technologies presents significant challenges for academic institutions to maintain current knowledge and skills.
  • Generative AI adoption in academia is accelerating rapidly, with universities increasingly expecting AI literacy from their staff.
  • Lack of AI competence is cited as a critical gap in higher education, affecting research quality and student preparation.
  • Learn how to integrate GenAI responsibly into research, teaching, and administrative tasks through use-case tutorials.

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for academic staff who wish to deepen practical AI skills. No coding background required.

Researchers & Faculty

Enhance research methodologies, data analysis, and academic writing with AI.

Teaching Staff

Integrate AI effectively into curriculum design and student assessment.

Administrative Staff

Streamline administrative processes and improve institutional efficiency.

PhD Students

Leverage AI for research design, literature reviews, and academic writing.

What You'll Learn & Achieve

Key Learning Outcomes:

  • Use GenAI safely & effectively for research, teaching, and administrative tasks.
  • Map AI opportunities & ethical considerations in academic contexts.
  • Design smart prompts for literature reviews, data analysis, and academic writing.
  • Guide students and colleagues through AI adoption in educational settings.
  • Contribute to institutional AI policies and academic integrity guidelines.
  • Learn tips and tutorials in science communication.

Certification & Compliance:

  • Successful participants receive a verifiable Chalmers Micro-Credential (5 ECTS-equivalent) and LinkedIn-ready badge.
  • Designed to ensure participants satisfy the EU AI Act conditions for responsible AI usage.
  • This course addresses academic integrity and responsible AI usage in higher education.
  • Digital diploma after completing set exercises and attending 1 live & peer session.
