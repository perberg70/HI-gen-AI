Experience the synergy of human creativity and AI power. This course empowers you to responsibly leverage Generative AI to unlock new ideas and drive innovation in your work.

Why This Course, Why Now?

  • Generative AI is already embedded in everyday workflows: 75% of knowledge-workers use GenAI tools, and two-thirds of managers now expect AI skills.
  • Lack of competence is cited by KPMG as an "enterprise-critical risk" for two-thirds of firms.
  • This practice-rich course equips staff to test, adopt and govern GenAI responsibly, turning curiosity into confident, value-adding use.

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for professionals who wish to deepen practical AI skills. No coding background required; post-secondary education recommended.

Academic (University Staff)

Enhance research, teaching, and administrative tasks with AI.

High School (Teachers & Employees)

Integrate AI effectively into education and school operations.

Industry Professionals

Drive innovation and efficiency across various sectors.

Engineers

Leverage AI for design, development, and problem-solving.

What You'll Learn & Achieve

Key Learning Outcomes:

  • Use GenAI safely & effectively for everyday work tasks.
  • Map AI opportunities & pitfalls in your own role (bias, privacy, etc.).
  • Design smart prompts & simple agents, build a basic custom chatbot.
  • Guide teams through change, understanding human factors and organizational adoption.
  • Plan next-step upskilling and contribute to an internal AI learning culture.

Certification & Compliance:

  • Successful participants receive a verifiable Chalmers Micro-Credential (5 ECTS-equivalent) and LinkedIn-ready badge.
  • This course is designed to ensure participants satisfy the EU AI Act conditions for responsible AI usage.
  • Digital diploma after completing set exercises and attending 1 live & peer session.
