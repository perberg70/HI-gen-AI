Supercharge Your Engineering Workflow with AI!

Experience the synergy of human creativity and the power of AI. This course empowers engineers to responsibly leverage Generative AI to enhance technical problem-solving, code development, and system design processes.

Why This Course, Why Now?

  • The rapid evolution of AI technologies is transforming software development, with many developers now using AI coding assistants.
  • Generative AI is revolutionizing technical workflows: from code generation to system design, documentation, and debugging.
  • Engineering teams face increasing pressure to deliver faster while maintaining code quality and system reliability.
  • This practice-rich course equips engineers to integrate GenAI responsibly into development, testing, and deployment processes.

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for engineers and technical professionals who wish to deepen practical AI skills. Basic programming knowledge helpful; engineering background recommended.

Software Engineers

Enhance coding efficiency, debugging, and code review processes with AI.

System Architects

Leverage AI for system design, optimization, and technical documentation.

DevOps Engineers

Streamline deployment, monitoring, and infrastructure management with AI.

Technical Leads

Guide teams in adopting AI tools and establishing best practices.

What You'll Learn & Achieve

Key Learning Outcomes:

  • Use GenAI safely & effectively for code generation, debugging, and technical documentation.
  • Map AI opportunities & risks in software development and system design processes.
  • Design smart prompts for technical problem-solving and code optimization.
  • Integrate AI tools into CI/CD pipelines and development workflows.
  • Establish AI governance and best practices for engineering teams.

Certification & Compliance:

  • Successful participants receive a verifiable Chalmers Micro-Credential (5 ECTS-equivalent) and LinkedIn-ready badge.
  • Designed to ensure participants satisfy the EU AI Act conditions for responsible AI usage.
  • This course addresses AI ethics, security, and responsible development practices.
  • Digital diploma after completing set exercises and attending 1 live & peer session.
