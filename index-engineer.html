This course examines how AI is transforming engineering work tasks. Participants will learn to adapt to AI as an emerging technology and understand their evolving role as experts in an AI-enhanced engineering environment.

Why This Course, Why Now?

  • The rapid evolution of AI technologies is transforming engineering work tasks across all disciplines.
  • AI is revolutionizing technical workflows: from design optimization to documentation, analysis, and decision-making processes.
  • Engineering teams face increasing pressure to adapt to AI-enhanced work environments while maintaining professional standards.
  • This practice-rich course equips engineers to understand and adapt to AI as an emerging technology affecting their work.
  • Share experience and learn from others in the engineering community.

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for engineers and technical professionals who wish to develop practical AI skills. No coding background required.

Design Engineers

Adapt to AI-enhanced design processes and optimization workflows.

System Engineers

Understand AI integration in system design and technical documentation.

Project Engineers

Navigate AI-enhanced project management and decision-making processes.

Technical Leaders

Guide teams in adapting to AI as an emerging technology in engineering.
